Toppings

10 Terrific Cake Toppings

  • Fresh fruit, such as orange wheels and mixed berries
  • Mini chocolate candies (baking bits in peanut butter, chocolate, butterscotch), crushed peppermints, or colorful candies such as jelly beans
  • Moldable fondant (available at baking supply stores), cut into shapes with cookie cutters
  • Powdered sugar or cocoa dusted over doilies and stencils
  • Shredded or toasted coconut
  • Chopped, slivered, or toasted nuts
  • Small cookies such as animal crackers, pirouettes, or sugar cookies cut into shapes
  • Chocolate curls (scrape a chocolate bar with a vegetable peeler)
  • Candies in shapes such as ducks and fish (available at www.wilton.com)
  • Spun-sugar flowers (available at www.sweetc.com)
To create a water bath , use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the cake is placed .
