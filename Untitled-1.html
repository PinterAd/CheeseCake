Cake Tips and Techniques

Professional Tips

When baking a cheesecake , it is important not to over bake it . You only want to bake it until the middle has a slight wiggle , not until it is rock solid .

It is important that you use a water bath , discussed at the right , to ensure even baking of your cheesecake .

To create a water bath , use a pan that will allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the cake is placed .

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C/ fan 160C/ gas 4.
  2. Blend the digestive biscuits and the butter in a food processor until the mixture looks like wet sand. Then, press the mixture into the bottom of the tin (20cm) and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Cool the base of the cheesecake while preparing the filling.
  4. Stir in a bowl the soft cheese and the caster sugar for a little. Continue by adding the double cream. Pour the mixture in the tin and smooth with a spoon or a spatula.
  5. Leave to set in the fridge for 1 hour or better overnight and serve with strawberries or rasberries or any marmelade you like.
